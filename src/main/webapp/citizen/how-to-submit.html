How to Submit a Grievance

Learn how to effectively submit a grievance with proper details for faster resolution

Follow these steps for best results

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Prepare Your Information

Before starting, gather all necessary information and documents related to your grievance.

  • Your personal details (name, contact information)
  • Specific location of the issue (address, landmark)
  • Date and time when the issue occurred or was noticed
  • Any relevant documents like photos
2

Select the Right Category

Choose the most appropriate category and department for your grievance to ensure it reaches the right authorities.

Common Categories:

Water Supply Electricity Road & Transport Sanitation
3

Provide Clear Details

Write a clear and concise description of your grievance with all relevant details.

✅ Good Example:

"Water supply has been irregular in Sector 15, Chandigarh for the past 5 days. Supply is available only for 1 hour in the morning (6-7 AM) and 1 hour in the evening (6-7 PM). This is affecting approximately 200 households in the area."

❌ Poor Example:

"No water in my area. Fix it soon."

Tips for Effective Description:

  • Be specific about location, dates, and duration
  • Mention how many people are affected
  • Describe the impact on daily life
  • Include any previous attempts to resolve the issue
4

Attach Supporting Documents

Upload relevant photos or documents that support your grievance.

Accepted File Types:

Images (JPG, PNG)

Maximum file size: 10MB per file

What to Include in Photos

  • Clear images showing the problem
  • Landmarks to identify location
  • Date and time stamps if possible
  • Any safety hazards visible
5

Review and Submit

Double-check all information before submitting your grievance.

Checklist Before Submitting:

  • All personal details are correct
  • Category and department are appropriate
  • Description is clear and complete
  • Location details are accurate
  • Supporting documents are attached
  • Contact information is correct for follow-up

After submission, you will receive a unique grievance ID for tracking. Save this ID for future reference.

Quick Tips for Success

Be Specific

Provide exact locations, dates, and details to help authorities address your issue faster.

Remain Civil

Use polite and respectful language. This increases the likelihood of a positive response.

Follow Up

Use your grievance ID to track progress and provide additional information if requested.

One Issue Per Grievance

Submit separate grievances for different issues to ensure each gets proper attention.
