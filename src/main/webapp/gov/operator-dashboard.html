Frequently Asked Questions

How do I assign a task to a worker?

Go to the Task Management section, find the unassigned task, and click the "Assign" button next to it.

How can I view worker performance?

Navigate to the Analytics section to see detailed performance metrics for each worker.

What is SLA compliance?

SLA (Service Level Agreement) compliance measures how well tasks are completed within their designated timeframes.
