The Grievance Redressal Process

A clear, time-bound workflow for efficient resolution.

STEP 1

Submit Grievance

Citizen registers the grievance with all necessary details.

STEP 2

Assign to Officer

Issue is automatically routed to the relevant nodal officer.

STEP 3

Resolution Action

Department takes action and submits a report with evidence.

STEP 4

Final Closure

Citizen is notified and provides feedback to close the case.
